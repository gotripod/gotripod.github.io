This is how we do it

Let's get started

We use the following six-step approach to all our projects.

See what we've done

Software development is a complicated business. We make it simpler through the use of a clearly defined process. Depending on the size of your project we can split it into phases and iterate through our process. Either way we have clear milestones and tangible deliverables at each step and you're free to utilise our services for as many or as few of our six steps that suits you.

Step 1
We discuss your idea

Pick up the phone and give us a buzz. Most of our projects start this way. (Though, if you prefer, we'd be happy to sign a non-disclosure-agreement before discussing things.) Once the formalities are out the way and we know a little about what it is you want to do and you know a little more about us we can arrange a meet-up to get down to business.

Time : Half an hour
Step 2
We workshop your project

You come to us or we'll come to you. We'll spend an informal day hammering out the details and implications of your idea. There'll be a whiteboard, sticky notes and coffee. Plenty of coffee. Usually sandwiches and cake too. This will give both of us a proper handle on your idea. After the workshop day we'll produce a summary document that covers what was discussed and outlines the functional requirements of your project and how these could be addressed in a phased development approach.

Time : One day
